Celebrate the Khmer New Year with a unique floating festival at Kampong Phluk. Join the community in traditional games, boat races, and special ceremonies on the water. Experience the vibrant culture and authentic lifestyle of the Tonle Sap Lake.

The village transforms into a lively venue for the Sangkran festivities. Visitors can witness the "Trot" dance, participate in water blessings, and enjoy local delicacies prepared specially for the new year celebration.
