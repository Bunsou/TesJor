# Project Proposal: TesJor

**"Unlock the Real Cambodia, One Quest at a Time"**

## 1. The Concept in Brief

**TesJor** is a digital travel companion designed to solve the biggest problem in Cambodian tourism: **"Where do I go after Angkor Wat?"**

Instead of a boring directory of places, we turn travel into a **game**. We provide tourists with curated "checklists" of hidden villages, authentic foods, and local events. Users explore the country, check off items on their digital passport, and earn badges for discovering the "real" Cambodia.

It is accessible immediately via any web browser on a phone‚Äîno app download required.

---

## 2. The Problem

While Cambodia is rich in culture, most tourism is concentrated in a few small areas (mainly the temples of Siem Reap).

- **The "Tourist Bubble":** Visitors often stick to the main path because they lack information about safe, interesting places in rural areas.
- **Missed Economic Opportunity:** Small villages, local artisans, and family-run food stalls miss out on tourism revenue because no one knows they exist.
- **Lack of Guidance:** Tourists want authentic experiences (like "eating real street food") but don't know what to order or where to find it.

## 3. Our Solution: The "Quest" System

We are building a platform that guides tourists to these hidden gems by making it fun and organized.

### How it Works for the User:

1.  **Instant Access:** The user opens our website link. It looks and feels exactly like a mobile app, but they don't need to waste time downloading anything from an App Store. They sign in instantly with their **Gmail** account.
2.  **Discover:** They browse curated categories like _"Hidden Villages," "Must-Try Khmer Drinks,"_ or _"Traditional Crafts."_
3.  **Plan:** They can **Bookmark** places they want to visit or foods they want to try, creating a personal itinerary.
4.  **Navigate:** They use our **Interactive Map** to see hidden gems near their current location.
5.  **Complete the Quest:** When they visit a place or eat a dish, they tap **"Check-in"** on the app. This marks it as complete in their history, filling up their "Digital Passport."

## 5. The Impact

This project is about more than just a holiday; it‚Äôs about **distributing value.**

- **For the Tourist:** They get a unique, authentic story to tell back home, far beyond the standard temple photos.
- **For the Locals:** We drive foot traffic to small businesses, homestays, and street vendors who usually don't have marketing budgets.
